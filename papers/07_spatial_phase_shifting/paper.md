## Abstract

Standard models of teleportation (Level 3) rely on the transfer of quantum states via classical channels and entangled pairs, often constrained by sub-light propagation limits and the "no-cloning" theorem. In the Afolabi Unified Framework (AUF), teleportation is reframed as **Spatial Phase-Shifting**. This paper argues that spatial coordinates are not fundamental but are metadata attributes of informational patterns within the Field (Φ). We propose that by manipulating the **Informational Address** of a coherent pattern, a system can "re-manifest" at a distant coordinate without traversing the intermediate spacetime manifold. We derive the transition probability for "Phase Tunneling" and establish the requirements for high-fidelity re-manifestation.
