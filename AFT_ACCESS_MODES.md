# AFT Access Modes: Basic vs. Pro Tiering

The Afolabi Unified Framework (AUF) implements a tiered access model to ensure safety, security, and the controlled deployment of high-density manifestations.

| **Manifestation Level** | Level 1.0 - 2.5 | Level 1.0 - 4.5 |

## II. AFT Basic (Development Mode)

Designed for researchers, students, and curious observers.
- **Goal**: Conceptual understanding and theoretical validation.
- **Rendering**: Uses "Hollow-Lattice" technology. You can see the structure of reality, but you cannot "touch" it. 
- **Simulations**: Highly accurate, but restricted to the digital domain or low-density light fields.
